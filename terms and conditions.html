Pricing and Billing

  • All quoted prices are **exclusive of taxes**. Applicable taxes will be added to your final invoice.
  • A **5% Service Management fee** will be charged in addition to the overall monthly billing.
  • Travel charges are included for distances up to 50 km (to and from customer sites). Travel exceeding **50 km** will be charged at **$0.40 per km** in addition to the standard rates.
  • The provided prices are applicable only for work performed during **standard business hours on working days**.
  • For any out-of-hours break-fix support, charges will be **1.5 times** the standard estimated rate.
  • For break-fix support on **weekends and holidays**, charges will be **2 times** the standard estimated rate.

Site Visits and Cancellations

  • If a Teceze engineer visits a site and is **denied access**, the corresponding ticket/incident will be billed at the full rate for SBD, NBD, 2BD, 3BD, 4BD, or half/full-day rate for the respective country.
  • Cancellations of a scheduled Teceze engineer visit with **less than 24 hours' notice** will incur a **50% cancellation fee** from the corresponding SBD, NBD, 2BD, 3BD, 4BD, or half/full-day rate for the respective country.

Additional Costs and Scope of Work

  • **Transition costs** are additional and will be charged at actuals (Time and Materials rates) for a technician's travel to a customer site for any site knowledge acquisition required.
  • The prices provided are based on a **standard scope of work** for providing infrastructure L1 to L5 support for IT managed services, data center operations, digital workplace, network, and security. The final pricing may vary depending on specific customer requirements and the final scope of work.
